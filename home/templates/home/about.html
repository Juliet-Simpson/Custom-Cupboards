What We Do

Founded in 2020, we are a small family run firm specialising in the tailoring of fine bespoke wooden cupboards and shelving units made exactly to your specifications.

Head carpenter Barry has more than 20 years joinery experience and is now putting his passions for both skilled woodwork and for art into making his designs realities.

Our cupboards are first constructed to completion then meticulously dismantled and packed with detailed instructions for reassembly.

We are proud of our unique designs which will provide you with a truly remarkable piece of furniture that will fit perfectly where ever you have in mind for it.

It is our top priority that you are happy with the finished result and we are available on the phone 8am - 6pm weekdays and 9am -12 midday on Sat to offer support and answer any enquiries.
